What's it about?

BrewTrackr allows users to search for their favorite coffee and breweries. It can be hard to keep track of all the places you've been to, but with BrewTrackr you can store all your favourite spots in one place!

How does it work?

BrewTrackr features a user-friendly interface that allows you to add your favorite spots to your account. Once you sign up, you will be able to look up coffee and breweries by location and store them into your dashboard. Once you've added your spot, it will be stored in your dashboard for you to edit or delete whenever you choose.
